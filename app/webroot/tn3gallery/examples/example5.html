The gallery in the example above can be controlled externally by linking to #tn3-0-next to display the next image or #tn3-0-prev to display the previous image. The next image can also be displayed in fullscreen mode by linking to #tn3-0-next-fs

You can also create a direct link to display a specific image in a specific album. In this link, the first number represents the instance of the TN3 Gallery that's on the stage, the second is the album number and the third is the image. Here are some examples:

  • #tn3-0-0-3 will display the first gallery, first album and fourth image
  • #tn3-1-0-5-fs will display the second gallery (which is hidden), first album, sixth image in fullscreen mode

When the "fullOnly" property is set to "true", the TN3 Gallery will be hidden.
